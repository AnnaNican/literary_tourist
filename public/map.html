Literary Tourist

IToday travel is accessible and widely practiced across people independent of their demographic attributes. People travel for different reasons, so why not travel for places that store knowledge in book bindings? This app is made for those who want to travel for (or at least stop by ) at the places that carry books, either for sale, public access or have landmarks that have have been in some significance to authors.

** currently US only (hopefully more countries added soon)

BE ADVENTITIOUS

Sometimes it’s good to discover by accident rather than design. “Fly” into a random city to explore literally attractions.

BE CONSCIOUS

Instantaneous “zoom-out” to see the distribution of literally places as an overview. Marvel on how many we have.

BE SELECTIVE

Select specific type of literally destinations. Toggle between bookstores and libraries to make exploration more concentrated.

Other interactions

  • Use top-right search box to find a specific city
  • Hover over pin to get overview
  • Click on pin to open location’s website in new tab
